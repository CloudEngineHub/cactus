Key Settings: Temper 45°C → 27°C → 31.5°C; tunnel 10–12°C; hold if index < 5.0.

SOP (Spicy Bark, ultra-brief): 1) Sanitize. 2) Temper ≥ 5.0. 3) Deposit + 0.8% spice. 4) Cool ≤ 18°C. 5) Lot & sample.
